Scrimmage Sports

















Welcome To The Scrimmage Sports App!


This web app will show you game scoreboards, team standings, and game schedules from all the professional leagues across different major sports.




NFL Scorecards


This section covers scorecards of past games from the 2016 regular season. Below are the results from each game played on a specific day, where the game occurred, and how many points each team scored in each quarter. In the drop down menu, you can browse through different weeks that contains regularly scheduled games played.


NFL Schedule


This section covers the complete schedule of past games from the 2016 regular season sorted by date. It tells us what matchups occurred, the time game was scheduled to play, and in what sports stadium the game occurred.



NFL Standings


This section covers team standings from the 2016 regular season sorted by winningest team and categorized by division. It tells us what was the win-loss-tie record for each team, total games played, total points for (meaning offensive scores), and total points against (meaning opponents score offensively against them). At the bottom of the page, you'll find a glossary for reading this table.

Glossary
W: Wins
L: Losses
T: Ties
GP: Games Played
PF - Total Points For
PA: Total Points Against
